# 📊 Business Intelligence & Sales Analytics Platform

An end-to-end Data Analytics project demonstrating the complete analytics lifecycle—from data extraction and cleaning to business intelligence reporting and dashboard visualization. This project analyzes customer shopping behavior to uncover purchasing patterns, customer segments, and sales trends that support data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

Businesses generate large volumes of transactional data but often struggle to convert it into meaningful insights. This project analyzes customer shopping behavior to answer key business questions such as:

- Which customer segments generate the highest revenue?
- What are the top-performing product categories?
- How do sales vary across regions and demographics?
- Which purchasing patterns can drive better business decisions?

The project combines SQL, Python, and Power BI to transform raw transactional data into actionable insights through interactive dashboards.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **SQL** – Data extraction, querying, and business analysis
- **Python** – Data cleaning, preprocessing, and exploratory data analysis
- **Pandas & NumPy** – Data manipulation
- **Power BI** – Interactive dashboards and KPI reporting
- **Microsoft Excel** – Initial data exploration

## 🔄 Project Workflow

### 1️⃣ Business Understanding
- Defined the business objectives and analytical questions.
- Identified key performance indicators (KPIs) for measuring business performance.

### 2️⃣ Data Preparation
- Imported and explored customer shopping data.
- Cleaned missing values and duplicate records.
- Standardized categorical fields and validated data quality.

### 3️⃣ SQL Analysis
- Wrote SQL queries to analyze customer behavior.
- Performed aggregations and filtering.
- Identified high-value customers, product trends, and revenue patterns.

### 4️⃣ Exploratory Data Analysis
- Used Python (Pandas) to analyze customer demographics and purchasing behavior.
- Generated summary statistics and visualizations.
- Identified trends and anomalies within the dataset.

### 5️⃣ Dashboard Development
- Built interactive Power BI dashboards.
- Designed KPI cards and trend visualizations.
- Enabled drill-down analysis using slicers and filters.
